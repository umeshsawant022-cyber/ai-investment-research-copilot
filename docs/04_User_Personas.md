# User Personas

## Purpose

This document defines the primary users of AI Investment Research Copilot, their goals, challenges, and how the platform helps them perform investment research more efficiently.

---

# Persona 1: Retail Investor

## Profile

- Age: 25–45
- Investment Experience: Beginner to Intermediate
- Investment Style: Long-term investing
- Frequency: Weekly or Monthly

### Goals

- Research companies before investing
- Understand financial performance
- Identify investment risks
- Make informed investment decisions

### Pain Points

- Financial reports are difficult to understand
- Information is spread across multiple websites
- Limited time for research
- Uncertainty about interpreting technical indicators

### How the Platform Helps

- Summarizes financial reports
- Explains technical indicators in simple language
- Consolidates news and company information
- Generates easy-to-understand investment research reports

---

# Persona 2: Financial Analyst

## Profile

- Works in financial services
- Performs equity research
- Reviews multiple companies daily

### Goals

- Reduce manual research effort
- Increase productivity
- Standardize investment reports
- Validate investment hypotheses quickly

### Pain Points

- Repetitive analysis
- Time-consuming report preparation
- Switching between multiple tools
- Manual comparison of financial metrics

### How the Platform Helps

- Automates research workflows
- Summarizes company filings
- Provides structured analysis
- Generates consistent research reports

---

# Persona 3: Portfolio Manager

## Profile

- Manages investment portfolios
- Evaluates multiple sectors
- Makes strategic allocation decisions

### Goals

- Monitor portfolio companies
- Identify emerging risks
- Compare investment opportunities
- Review market trends efficiently

### Pain Points

- Large volume of information
- Keeping up with market developments
- Comparing multiple companies
- Tracking investment risks

### How the Platform Helps

- Consolidates company insights
- Highlights key financial changes
- Summarizes market developments
- Supports faster portfolio reviews

---

# Persona 4: Investment Advisor

## Profile

- Advises clients on investment opportunities
- Conducts investment research
- Prepares client presentations

### Goals

- Deliver high-quality investment insights
- Save research time
- Improve consistency
- Explain investment recommendations clearly

### Pain Points

- Preparing client reports manually
- Gathering information from multiple sources
- Explaining complex financial concepts
- Maintaining consistent research quality

### How the Platform Helps

- Produces explainable investment research
- Generates structured reports
- Highlights risks and opportunities
- Supports transparent client discussions

---

# Common User Needs

Across all personas, users want to:

- Save research time
- Access reliable financial information
- Understand company performance
- Identify investment risks
- Receive explainable AI insights
- Make informed investment decisions

---

# Design Principles

The product is designed to:

- Assist—not replace—human decision-making
- Present transparent and explainable AI outputs
- Reduce repetitive research tasks
- Consolidate information from multiple sources
- Support consistent investment analysis

---

# Persona Summary

| Persona | Primary Goal | Key Challenge |
|----------|--------------|---------------|
| Retail Investor | Make informed investment decisions | Limited financial knowledge and fragmented information |
| Financial Analyst | Improve research productivity | Manual and repetitive analysis |
| Portfolio Manager | Monitor investments efficiently | Large volume of data and market changes |
| Investment Advisor | Deliver quality client insights | Time-consuming research and reporting |
