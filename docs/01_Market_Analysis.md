# Market Analysis

## Product Overview

AI Investment Research Copilot is an AI-powered investment research platform designed to help investors and financial professionals analyze companies by combining financial statements, technical indicators, market data, company filings, and news into a single explainable research workflow.

Rather than replacing human decision-making, the platform assists users by automating repetitive research tasks and providing transparent, AI-generated insights.

---

# Market Opportunity

Investment research requires reviewing information from multiple sources, including:

- Annual Reports
- Quarterly Earnings Reports
- Financial Statements
- Stock Price History
- Technical Indicators
- Market News
- Industry Trends
- Company Announcements

Gathering and interpreting this information manually is time-consuming and often inconsistent.

Recent advances in Generative AI and Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) create opportunities to streamline research while improving productivity and decision support.

# Business Challenges

Current investment research often involves:

- Switching between multiple financial websites
- Reading lengthy annual and quarterly reports
- Monitoring news from different sources

These activities increase research time and reduce consistency.
