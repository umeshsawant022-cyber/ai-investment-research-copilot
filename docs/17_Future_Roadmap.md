# Future Roadmap

## Purpose

This document outlines the long-term product vision for AI Investment Research Copilot beyond the MVP. It highlights potential capabilities that can enhance investment research, improve user experience, and support enterprise-scale adoption.

---

# Product Vision

Become an intelligent AI-powered investment research platform that helps investors and financial professionals analyze companies, monitor portfolios, and make informed investment decisions through explainable AI.

---

# Product Evolution

## Version 1.0 — MVP

### Objective

Deliver an end-to-end AI-assisted investment research workflow.

### Capabilities

- Company Search
- Market Data Analysis
- Financial Statement Analysis
- Technical Analysis
- News Summarization
- AI Investment Research Report
- Confidence Score
- Human Review

---

## Version 2.0 — Enhanced AI Research

### Objective

Improve the quality and depth of AI-generated insights.

### New Features

- Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG)
- Annual Report Analysis
- Quarterly Earnings Analysis
- Earnings Call Transcript Summarization
- Source Citations
- Enhanced Explainability
- Improved Confidence Scoring

---

## Version 3.0 — Portfolio Intelligence

### Objective

Support investors managing multiple investments.

### New Features

- Portfolio Dashboard
- Watchlists
- Portfolio Performance Tracking
- Risk Monitoring
- Company Comparison
- Sector Analysis
- Investment Thesis Builder

---

## Version 4.0 — Enterprise Platform

### Objective

Support enterprise investment research teams.

### New Features

- Team Collaboration
- Report Sharing
- Role-Based Access Control
- Audit Logs
- Enterprise Authentication (SSO)
- Workflow Management
- Cloud Deployment

---

## Version 5.0 — AI Investment Intelligence

### Objective

Expand AI capabilities with predictive insights.

### New Features

- Personalized Research Assistant
- Multi-LLM Support
- AI Portfolio Insights
- Predictive Trend Analysis
- Macroeconomic Analysis
- ESG Analysis
- Smart Alerts & Notifications

---

# Long-Term Vision

Future versions of the platform may support:

- Multi-Asset Analysis (Stocks, ETFs, Mutual Funds)
- Global Market Coverage
- Dividend Analysis
- Options Strategy Insights
- AI Chat Interface
- Voice-Based Investment Assistant
- Mobile Application
- Real-Time Market Monitoring
- Enterprise Knowledge Integration

---

# Technology Evolution

| Stage | Technology Focus |
|--------|------------------|
| MVP | FastAPI, Streamlit, OpenAI GPT, Financial APIs |
| Enhanced AI | RAG, OpenAI Embeddings, ChromaDB |
| Enterprise | Cloud Deployment, Authentication, Monitoring |
| Advanced AI | Multi-Agent Orchestration, Multi-LLM Support |
| Future | Predictive Analytics, Personalized AI, Global Market Intelligence |

---

# Product Goals

The long-term roadmap aims to:

- Improve investment research productivity.
- Deliver explainable AI-driven insights.
- Support scalable enterprise adoption.
- Enable intelligent portfolio monitoring.
- Expand coverage across financial markets and asset classes.

---

# Success Metrics

The future roadmap will be considered successful if it achieves:

- Increased user adoption
- Reduced research effort
- Improved AI report quality
- Higher user satisfaction
- Enterprise customer adoption
- Expansion into new markets and use cases

---

# Guiding Principles

The product will continue to evolve based on the following principles:

- User-centric design
- Explainable AI
- Responsible AI
- Human-in-the-loop decision support
- Modular architecture
- Scalable platform design
- Continuous innovation
