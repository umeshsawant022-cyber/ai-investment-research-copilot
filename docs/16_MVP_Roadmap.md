# MVP Roadmap

## Purpose

This roadmap outlines the phased delivery plan for AI Investment Research Copilot. It defines the capabilities planned for the MVP and the subsequent enhancements that will evolve the platform into an enterprise-grade AI investment research solution.

---

# Product Vision

Build an AI-powered investment research platform that enables investors and financial professionals to generate explainable, data-driven investment research by combining financial analysis, technical analysis, market intelligence, and Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG).

---

# Phase 1 – MVP

## Goal

Deliver an end-to-end AI-assisted investment research workflow.

### Features

- Company Search
- Market Data Retrieval
- Financial Statement Analysis
- Technical Indicator Analysis
- News Summarization
- AI Investment Research Report
- Confidence Score
- Human Review Workflow

### Deliverables

- Streamlit UI
- FastAPI Backend
- Workflow Orchestrator
- AI Agent Framework
- Investment Research Report

### Success Criteria

- End-to-end workflow operational
- Research reports generated successfully
- Positive user feedback on report quality

---

# Phase 2 – AI Enhancements

## Goal

Improve the depth and reliability of AI-generated insights.

### Features

- Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG)
- Annual Report Analysis
- Quarterly Earnings Analysis
- Earnings Call Transcript Summarization
- Enhanced News & Sentiment Analysis
- Improved Confidence Scoring

### Deliverables

- ChromaDB integration
- OpenAI Embeddings
- Enterprise knowledge retrieval
- Source-grounded AI responses

### Success Criteria

- Improved report quality
- Reduced hallucinations
- Better explainability

---
